C. Supported Platforms¶

Phlex must support the platforms that are required by its stakeholders. As of this writing, Phlex programs must run on the AlmaLinux 9 operating system, which is used for DUNE’s batch-processing needs.

Where practicable, Phlex may be usable on other systems, such as Ubuntu and macOS. However, any platforms not required by Phlex’s stakeholders (or non-essential platforms) will be supported on a best-effort basis. In addition, the set of supportable non-essential platforms is constrained by the libraries required by Phlex and the available compute nodes provided by continuous integration systems.
